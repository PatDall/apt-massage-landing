Relaxation/Swedish/Theraputic Massage


Relaxation/Swedish/Therapeutic Massage techniques are a great choice if you suffer from:

  • Insomnia
  • Stress and stress-related disorders
  • Anxiety
  • Depression and other mood disorders
  • Circulatory issues

This type of massage is purely to relax with benefits of increasing oxygen in the blood, removal of waste product by increasing circulation, and gently reduces muscle tension. Relaxation massages can be combined with other massages, that is then primarily to warm up muscles and tissues before apply a more specific massage technique to correct and/or maintain the pain/tension/injury.

Please note that health fund rebates will not apply to this type of massage.
