The Pain Relief Experts

Activate Health & Fitness is passionate about your wellbeing. Whether you're new to exercise or an experienced athlete, we love helping our clients get the most out of their sports performance and fitness goals.

As experienced massage therapists working in a boutique health and fitness studio in the middle of Brisbane city,we understand the stresses and strains that busy work schedules and exercise can have on the body.

Our therapists treat all kinds of pain, including:

  • general muscle pain
  • sports related injuries
  • tension headaches
  • chronic pain

Whatever your issues, our therapists can improve performance, aid recovery, prevent and eliminate injuries, combat stress and even enhance your metabolism and circulation.
